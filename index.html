Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 16, 2025

Welcome to Circlify, an AI-powered virtual try-on application with a community sharing feature. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices in accordance with applicable privacy laws including GDPR, CCPA, and Google Play Store requirements.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide you with our AI virtual try-on services and community features.

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address for authentication and account management
  • Profile Details: Optional information such as gender, age, and height to enhance AI recommendations and personalization
  • Images: Photos you upload or capture within the app for virtual try-on features
  • Community Posts: Try-on images, captions, and metadata you voluntarily share in our community feature

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features accessed, session duration, interaction patterns, crash reports, and performance diagnostics
  • Advertising Identifiers: Google Advertising ID (GAID) for personalized advertising through Google AdMob
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, app errors, and system activity
  • Community Engagement: Interactions with community posts (likes, views, engagement metrics)

1.3 AI-Powered Image Processing

How We Process Your Images for Virtual Try-On:

  • Third-Party AI Processing: Your uploaded images are sent to Google Gemini API for AI-powered virtual try-on generation
  • Temporary Transmission: Images are transmitted securely via HTTPS to Google's servers for processing
  • Google's Data Handling: Google may temporarily retain your images for up to 24 hours in-memory cache for performance optimization and abuse monitoring purposes as per their Gemini API Terms
  • Not Stored by Circlify: We do NOT store your original uploaded images or generated try-on results on our servers or Firebase
  • Local Device Storage: Generated try-on images are saved only on your device and remain under your control
  • Not Used for AI Training: Per Google's Gemini API policies, your images are not used to train or improve AI models
  • Community Sharing Exception: Only images you explicitly choose to share via the "Create Post" feature are uploaded and stored in Firebase

1.4 Universal Try-On Feature (MediaProjection & Overlay)

Zero Data Collection Guarantee:

  • Screen Recording (MediaProjection): When you use the universal try-on feature with screen recording permissions, we do NOT collect, store, or upload any screen content, bitmaps, or recorded data
  • Overlay Circles: The overlay circles displayed on your screen are rendered locally on your device in real-time. We do NOT capture, save, or transmit any bitmap or visual data from these overlays
  • On-Device Rendering: All universal try-on overlay processing happens entirely on your device with zero server transmission
  • No Server Access: We have no technical capability to access, view, or store content displayed through MediaProjection or overlay features
  • Privacy by Design: These features operate entirely locally for maximum privacy protection

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Send images to Google Gemini API for AI-powered virtual try-on generation and deliver results to your device
  • Local Storage: Save generated try-on results directly to your device for private viewing
  • Universal Try-On: Enable on-device screen overlay and try-on visualization without data collection
  • Community Features: Display user-shared posts, enable social interactions, and build an engaging community
  • Account Management: Authenticate users, manage subscriptions, and enforce usage limits
  • Personalization: Tailor AI recommendations based on your profile preferences
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features, fix bugs, and optimize performance
  • Communication: Send transactional notifications, service updates, and promotional messages (with your consent)
  • Advertising: Display relevant ads through Google AdMob and facilitate paid brand promotions
  • Monetization: With your explicit consent, anonymize and aggregate community engagement data for brand partnership insights
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations, enforce terms of service, and protect user safety

3. What We Do NOT Collect or Store

To ensure maximum transparency, here is what we explicitly do NOT collect or store:

  • Original Uploaded Images: Not stored on our servers or Firebase (sent only to Google Gemini API)
  • Generated Try-On Images: These remain only on your device unless you explicitly share to community
  • Screen Recording Data: No bitmaps, screenshots, or recorded content from MediaProjection permissions
  • Overlay Visual Data: No visual data from overlay circles or drawing features
  • Location Data: We do not track or collect your GPS location
  • Contact Lists: We do not access your device contacts
  • Phone Calls or SMS: We do not access call logs or text messages
  • Microphone Audio: We do not record or access audio

4. Community Sharing and Data Monetization

Transparency About Community Data

When you share try-on images to our community, you should be aware of the following:

  • Public Visibility: Posts you create are visible to all Circlify users and may appear in public feeds
  • Data Storage: Community posts (images, captions, engagement metrics) are stored in Firebase and remain there until you delete them
  • Brand Partnerships: We partner with fashion brands for promotional opportunities and advertising
  • Aggregated Analytics: We may share aggregated, anonymized community engagement statistics (e.g., trending styles, popular products) with brand partners to improve their services
  • Opt-In for Data Sharing: You will be asked for explicit consent if we wish to share your specific community post data or engagement patterns with brands for marketing insights
  • Paid Promotions: Brands may pay for promoted content or sponsored posts within the community. These will always be clearly labeled
  • Your Control: You can delete your community posts at any time, and you can opt out of data sharing for marketing purposes without losing access to core app features

4.1 Your Consent Rights for Data Monetization

Under GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy laws, we must obtain your informed consent before sharing your personal data for commercial purposes:

  • Explicit Opt-In: You will receive a clear consent request explaining exactly what data will be shared, with whom, and for what purpose
  • Granular Control: You can consent to some data uses while declining others
  • Easy Withdrawal: You may revoke consent at any time through app settings or by contacting us

  • Legitimate Interests: Improving app functionality, preventing fraud, ensuring security, and displaying relevant advertisements
  • Legal Obligation: Complying with applicable laws and regulations

6. How We Store and Protect Your Data

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption protocols when sending images to Google Gemini API
  • Secure Cloud Storage: Account data and community posts are stored in Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) with encryption at rest
  • Local Device Storage: Generated try-on images, MediaProjection data, and overlay rendering remain on your device under your control, using Android secure storage mechanisms
  • No Server Storage of Images: We do not store uploaded or generated images on our servers
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access on a need-to-know basis with authentication requirements
  • Regular Audits: Ongoing security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Third-Party Security: All service providers (Firebase, Google Gemini API, AdMob) maintain SOC 2 Type II or equivalent certifications

7. Third-Party Data Processing: Google Gemini API

Important Information About Google's Data Handling:

When you use Circlify's AI try-on features, your images are processed by Google Gemini API. Here's what you need to know:

  • Data Retention: Google may retain your images temporarily (up to 24 hours in-memory cache) for abuse monitoring and service optimization purposes
  • Not Used for Training: According to Google's Gemini API policies, your data is not used to train or improve Google's AI models
  • Abuse Monitoring: Google retains prompts and outputs for 55 days for abuse prevention and safety monitoring
  • Privacy Controls: Google's data handling is governed by their Gemini API Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
  • Data Location: Your images are processed on Google Cloud Platform infrastructure, which may be located outside your country
  • Your Rights: You can request deletion of your data from Google by contacting us, and we will coordinate with Google accordingly

By using Circlify's AI try-on features, you acknowledge that your images will be temporarily processed by Google Gemini API under these terms.
