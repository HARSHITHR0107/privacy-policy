How It Works - Technical Details

Q: What happens when I activate CircleTry mode?
When you activate CircleTry mode, the app displays a transparent drawing canvas over your screen. We do NOT capture or decode the underlying screen content. Only your finger gesture coordinates are recorded to extract the circular region you draw. Think of it like placing a piece of tracing paper over your screen - we only see what you trace, not what's underneath.
Q: What exactly gets sent to AI when I circle clothing?
Only the cropped image within your circle is sent to Google Gemini API - never the full screenshot. For example, if you circle a dress on Instagram, we extract only that dress region (approximately 200x200 to 800x800 pixels depending on your circle size) and send that isolated image for AI processing. Everything outside your circle remains private and is never transmitted.
Q: Where is my pose photo stored?
Your full-body pose photo is stored only on your device and sent to Gemini API each time you generate a try-on. We do not store your pose photo on our servers or in Firebase. You can update or delete your pose photo at any time in Settings, and it will be removed immediately from your device.
Q: What happens if I accidentally circle something inappropriate?
Before generating a try-on, we use Gemini 2.0 Flash Lite to validate the image. If it fails our safety check (NSFW content, multiple items, invalid clothing, lingerie, etc.), it is immediately discarded and never sent to Gemini 2.5 Flash for try-on generation. Google does not retain failed validation images. This protects both you and us from wasted AI costs.
Q: Does MediaProjection permission mean you're recording my screen?
No. MediaProjection allows us to display the overlay drawing canvas, but we have zero technical capability to record, capture, or decode your screen content. We only receive the mathematical coordinates of your finger movements to calculate the circle boundary. We cannot see your passwords, messages, banking apps, or any other screen content. This is a privacy-by-design architecture - even if we wanted to record your screen, our code doesn't have that functionality.

Universal Try-On: Circle any clothing item from any app (Instagram, Pinterest, shopping sites) and see yourself wearing it using AI.

How it works:

Google Gemini API Processing

Two-Stage AI System:

  • Stage 1 - Safety Check: Gemini 2.0 Flash Lite validates clothing images. Failed images are discarded immediately.
  • Stage 2 - Try-On Generation: Gemini 2.5 Flash combines your pose photo + validated clothing for photorealistic results.

Google's Data Retention:

  • Images retained for up to 55 days for abuse monitoring

By using Circlify, you acknowledge images will be processed by Google Gemini API under these terms. See Gemini API Terms.
